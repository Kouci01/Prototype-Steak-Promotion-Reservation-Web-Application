Wagyu Premium Collection offers you our Premium collection of Wagyu steaks that comes from Australia. Wagyu steaks that come from Japan are more expensive so we want to make our Wagyu Premium Collection more affordable but still have the same qualities like the Japanese Wagyu have. Australian Wagyu's marbling are rated with MB, and we choose MB6+ because the marbling of MB6 is already comparable to Japanese Wagyu A3. Steak cuts that we provide for Wagyu Premium Collection are Wagyu Sirloin, Wagyu Tenderloin, and Wagyu Ribeye.
